| Unfall premium (EUR 180) | EUR 0 deductible | Cap already used |
| KFZ-Haftpflicht premium (EUR 250) | EUR 0 deductible | Cap already used |

**For self-employed (EUR 2,800 cap):**

| Component | Annual Amount | Deductible? |
|-----------|--------------|-------------|
| PKV/GKV premium (Basisabsicherung) | ~EUR 6,000-12,000 | Yes, but exceeds cap |

**Bottom line:** For virtually all German residents, health insurance alone exhausts the sonstige Vorsorgeaufwendungen cap. All other insurance premiums in this category have zero practical tax benefit.

### Werbungskosten-Deductible Insurance Portions

These bypass the Vorsorgeaufwendungen cap because they're classified as work-related expenses:

| Insurance | Deductible Portion | Typical Amount | Tax Saved (at 35% rate) |
|-----------|--------------------|----------------|------------------------|
| Rechtsschutz (Beruf module) | 40-60% of premium | EUR 120-340/year | EUR 42-119/year |
| Hausrat (home office share) | Proportional to office sqm | EUR 15-40/year | EUR 5-14/year |
| KFZ (business use share) | Per Fahrtenbuch or Pauschale | Varies widely | Varies |
| Unfallversicherung (work portion) | ~50% if claimed as Werbungskosten | EUR 42-90/year | EUR 15-32/year |

## Cost Trend Indicators

### Premiums That Increase With Age
- **PKV:** Premiums rise significantly with age and medical inflation
- **BU:** Entry premium is locked, but entering later means higher locked-in premium
- **Zahnzusatz:** Entry premium is age-based, and some policies have age-based increases
- **KFZ:** Decreases with experience (SF-Klasse) but increases for elderly drivers

### Premiums That Stay Relatively Stable
- **GKV:** Income-based, not age-based (only increases with salary growth)
- **Haftpflicht:** Minimal age impact, inflation adjustments only
- **Rechtsschutz:** Some inflation adjustment, no significant age factor
- **Hausrat:** Stable unless coverage amount increases
- **Reisekranken:** Minimal age impact below 65, surcharges for 65+
- **Risikoleben:** Entry premium is locked for the term

### Cost Optimization Strategies
1. **Get BU and dental early:** Entry age locks in the premium. Every year you wait costs more.
2. **Annual KFZ comparison:** Switch every 1-2 years during the November Stichtag.
3. **Bundle cautiously:** Some providers offer multi-policy discounts, but don't sacrifice coverage quality for a 5% bundle discount.
4. **Increase deductibles:** Higher Selbstbeteiligung (EUR 150-500) reduces premiums 10-25%. Good for policies you rarely claim on.
5. **Annual vs monthly payment:** Many insurers offer 5-10% discount for annual payment vs monthly.
