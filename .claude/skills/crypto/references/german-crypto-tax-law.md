# German Crypto Tax Law — Reference

Authoritative sources:
- Einkommensteuergesetz (EStG) §22 Nr.3, §23
- BMF letter dated 10.05.2022
- BMF letter dated 06.03.2025

## §23 EStG — Private Veräußerungsgeschäfte

**Holding period:** 1 year. Disposal after 365 days → tax-free. Before → taxable.

**Freigrenze:**
- 2021–2023: €600/year
- From 2024: €1,000/year

If annual net §23 gain < Freigrenze: entire amount tax-free.
If ≥ Freigrenze: all of it is taxable.

## §22 Nr.3 EStG — Sonstige Einkünfte

Staking rewards, Simple Earn interest, airdrops with Leistung.
Freigrenze: €256/year.

## BMF 06.03.2025 clarifications

- Simple Earn Subscription/Redemption: NOT §23 realizations.
- Simple Earn Interest, Staking Rewards: §22 Nr.3 at FMV.
- 1-year holding period continues to apply.
