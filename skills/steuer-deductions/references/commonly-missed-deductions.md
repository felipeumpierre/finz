# Commonly Missed Deductions in Germany

**Last verified:** 2026-04-17
**Tax year anchor:** 2025

This reference lists deductions that many taxpayers overlook. Use it during the deductions interview to proactively ask about these items.

## Frequently Overlooked Werbungskosten

**Kontoführungsgebühren (Bank account fees)**
- 16€ flat rate is generally accepted without proof
- If actual fees are higher, use the real amount

**Telefon & Internet (Phone and internet costs)**
- Work-use portion is deductible (typically 20-50% for employees)
- 20% flat rate (up to 20€/month = 240€/year) is generally accepted
- Higher percentages need documentation (e.g., detailed phone bill analysis)

**Fachliteratur (Professional literature)**
- Books, journals, online subscriptions related to your profession
- Newspaper subscriptions if professionally required (e.g., journalist)

**Bewerbungsfotos (Application photos)**
- If you applied for jobs, professional photos are deductible

**Umzugskosten (Moving costs for work)**
- If you moved for a new job or to shorten commute significantly (>1 hour saved daily)
- Umzugskostenpauschale for sonstige Umzugsauslagen (unchanged for 2025 — still governed by BMF-Schreiben 28.12.2023, IV C 5 - S 2353/20/10004 :003, effective for moves from 1 March 2024 onward):
  - **964 EUR** for the Berechtigte (the relocating employee)
  - **+ 643 EUR** for each weitere mitziehende Person (spouse, each child)
  - **193 EUR** if the relocating person had no own Wohnung before the move (and set up none after)
  - Source: BMF-Schreiben 28.12.2023, reflected in the Amtliches Lohnsteuer-Handbuch 2025 Anhang 29 (<https://esth.bundesfinanzministerium.de/lsth/2025/B-Anhaenge/Anhang-29/III/inhalt.html>, retrieved 2026-04-17). No BMF update for 2025 had been issued as of April 2026 — the 1 March 2024 values remain in force.
- Plus actual costs: moving company, double rent during overlap, travel to viewings

**Arbeitsmittel (Work tools) — small items often forgotten**
- USB drives, cables, desk lamp, office supplies
- Work bag/briefcase
- Protective clothing/work uniform (but NOT regular business attire)

**Digitale Wirtschaftsgüter — Sofortabschreibung (1-Jahres-Nutzungsdauer)**
- Per BMF-Schreiben 26.02.2021 (IV C 3 - S 2190/21/10002 :013), clarified by BMF-Schreiben 22.02.2022 (IV C 3 - S 2190/21/10002 :025), taxpayers may apply a Nutzungsdauer of **1 year** to "Computerhardware" (desktops, notebooks, tablets, peripherals) and to operating/application software used for Dateneingabe und -verarbeitung, regardless of acquisition cost. This results in full depreciation in the year of purchase (Sofortabschreibung).
- Applies to acquisitions from 1 January 2021 onward and remains in force for tax year 2025 (no subsequent BMF reversal identified as of 2026-04-17).
- Source: <https://datenbank.nwb.de/Dokument/939719/> (BMF 26.04.2022 consolidating the rule). Commentary at <https://www.haufe.de/finance/jahresabschluss-bilanzierung/nutzungsdauer-von-computer-und-software_188_538794.html>.
- Practical consequence for employees: a 2,400 EUR work laptop bought in 2025 and used primarily for work can be written off entirely against 2025 Werbungskosten instead of being spread over 3 years. Private-use share must still be deducted. For items ≤ 800 EUR net the older GWG-Sofortabschreibung (§6 Abs. 2 EStG) already allows this, but the digitale-Wirtschaftsgüter rule removes the 800 EUR ceiling.

## Frequently Overlooked Sonderausgaben

**Steuerberatungskosten (Tax preparation costs)**
- Cost of tax software (WISO, SteuerSparErklärung, etc.) — partially deductible
- Steuerberater fees — deductible to the extent they relate to income determination
- Lohnsteuerhilfeverein membership fees — deductible

**Schuldgeldzahlungen (Private school fees)**
- 30% of school fees for recognized private schools, max 5,000€ per child
- Does NOT include boarding costs, meals, or tutoring

## Frequently Overlooked §35a Items

**From the Nebenkostenabrechnung (rental utility bill)**
Many tenants don't realize they can claim portions of their Nebenkosten:
- Hausmeister / Hauswart (caretaker)
- Gartenpflege (garden maintenance)
- Treppenhausreinigung (stairwell cleaning)
- Schornsteinfeger (chimney sweep)
- Aufzugswartung (elevator maintenance)
- Winterdienst (winter snow clearing)

The landlord is required to provide a §35a-Bescheinigung on request (or it's noted in the Nebenkostenabrechnung).

**IT Support at home**
- Computer setup, network installation, smart home setup by a technician → Handwerkerleistung

**Schornsteinfeger (Chimney sweep)**
- Measurement and inspection fees → Handwerkerleistung
- If you own a home, this is an easy one to forget

## Frequently Overlooked Außergewöhnliche Belastungen

**Krankheitskosten (Medical costs) people forget to add up:**
- Zuzahlungen for medications (pharmacy co-pays)
- Praxisgebühr (if still applicable)
- Fahrtkosten to doctors/hospitals (0.30€/km or public transport costs)
- Alternative medicine IF prescribed by a doctor (Heilpraktiker with Verordnung)
- Brille/Kontaktlinsen (glasses/contacts) with prescription
- Zahnreinigung (professional teeth cleaning) — controversial but sometimes accepted

**Tip:** Many people don't bother because individual costs are small, but they add up. Keep a running total throughout the year.

## Special Situations Often Missed

**Progressionsvorbehalt income**
- Elterngeld, Krankengeld, Arbeitslosengeld I, Kurzarbeitergeld, Mutterschaftsgeld
- These are tax-free but increase the tax rate on other income
- If one spouse received Elterngeld, Einzelveranlagung might be better (run the comparison!)

**Verlustverrechnung (Loss offsetting)**
- Losses from freelance work can offset employment income
- Losses from rental property can offset other income
- Unused losses can be carried forward (Verlustvortrag) or back (Verlustrücktrag, 1 year, max 10 Mio €)

**Riester for non-working spouse (mittelbar zulagenberechtigt)**
- If one spouse doesn't work but the other has Riester, the non-working spouse can also get a Riester contract with just 60€/year contribution and receive full Zulage (175€ + 185-300€ per child)
